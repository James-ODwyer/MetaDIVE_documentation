Welcome to MetaDIVE's documentation!
====================================

.. image:: images/MetaDive_image_idea_June_2024.png
   :alt: MetaDIVE logo
   :width: 400px
   :align: center

**MetaDIVE** is a metagenomics analysis pipeline which specialises in the high accuracy and low false negative detection.
of both **known** and highly **novel** viruses. MetaDIVE can be run in several ways depending on the modules chosen and 
the key goals of your project.
